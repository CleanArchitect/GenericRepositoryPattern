Intro DI

De 'problemen' (voorheen)
1 automatisering? (iedere service handmatig registreren)
2 bulky controllers (registratie per controller + grote constructors)
3 nothing is private (alle registraties via 1 project)

Voorbeeld voorheen
  Controller
  - injector method
  - constructor
  Startup
  - controller.injector

De 'oplossing' (huidig)     
1 assembly scanning
2 extension methods + fromservices
3 registratie per project + geen transitive dependencies

Voorbeeld:
API.Startup.ConfigureServices
   Gateways.Startup.ConfigureServices
	AddGateways -> scan IGateway (1)
	AddDbcontext
   UseCases.Startup.ConfigureServices
	AddInputPorts -> scan IInputPort<> (1)
	AddOutputPorts -> open IOutputPort<> (1)

Tips:
- fromservices
- open generics
- references: PrivateAssets="All" (nuget packages/entities)
